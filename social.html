JALS Social

JALS Social is the community outreach arm of J.A Living Spaces, committed to fostering sustainable, vibrant, resilient, and inclusive communities. We achieve this by delivering safe, innovative, and affordable housing solutions that are environmentally conscious, promoting long-term sustainability and equitable living.

As outlined in the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, access to adequate, safe, and affordable housing and basic services for all is a fundamental human right. At JALS Social, we recognize the urgent need to improve living conditions for the most vulnerable populations.

Uganda faces a severe housing crisis with a deficit of 2.4 million housing units. This crisis disproportionately affects marginalized groups, leading to homelessness, increased vulnerability, social exclusion, and limited access to essential services and opportunities. Inadequate infrastructure in these communities, such as limited access to clean water, sanitation facilities, and electricity, further exacerbates their living conditions and hinders socio-economic development. The rising costs of housing coupled with stagnant incomes make it increasingly difficult for these groups to afford decent and safe accommodation, perpetuating the cycle of poverty and inequality.

JALS Social aims to address this critical housing problem, focusing specifically on improving living conditions for marginalized communities. Our comprehensive housing improvement initiative is designed to alleviate the hardships faced by these communities. Our commitment to sustainability is central to our mission. We work towards establishing community-led initiatives and fostering partnerships with local stakeholders to create self-sustaining mechanisms that empower marginalized communities to thrive independently.
